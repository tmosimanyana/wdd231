About Us

The Kweneng Agriculture Chamber of Commerce is dedicated to supporting local agricultural businesses by providing resources, networking opportunities, and advocacy for the interests of our members.

Our Services

  • Networking opportunities for local farmers and agribusinesses
  • Access to resources and training programs
  • Information on agricultural events and workshops
  • Advocacy for agricultural policy and development in Kweneng
