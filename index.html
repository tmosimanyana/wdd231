Idaho

Idaho is a state in the Pacific Northwest region of the United States. It is known for its rugged landscapes, mountainous terrain, and vast outdoor recreation opportunities. Bordered by Montana and Wyoming to the east, Utah and Nevada to the south, and Washington and Oregon to the west, Idaho is famous for its potato farming, stunning national parks, and historic landmarks. Rexburg, a city in eastern Idaho, is home to Brigham Young University-Idaho, and is a hub for education and culture in the region.
