Kweneng Agriculture Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

1. Site Name

Site Name: Kweneng Agriculture Chamber of Commerce

Reason: Reflects the agricultural focus and the purpose of the chamber within the Kweneng region.

2. Site Purpose

The website will serve as a resource hub for agriculture-related businesses in the Kweneng region, offering:

  • A business directory.
  • Networking and collaboration opportunities.
  • Information on upcoming events and resources for members.

3. Scenarios

Here are two scenarios that reflect typical users:

  • Scenario 1: A business owner looking to join the chamber for networking and marketing support.
  • Scenario 2: A community member searching for upcoming agriculture events and workshops.

4. Color Schema

  • Primary Color: Dark Green (#006400) for the header and navigation.
  • Secondary Color: Gold (#FFD700) for buttons and links.
  • Background Color: Light Gray (#F5F5F5) for the main content background.

5. Typography

Font Family: Montserrat, sans-serif for all headings and body text.
