Kweneng Agriculture Chamber of Commerce

Site Plan

Site Name

Kweneng Agriculture Chamber of Commerce

The Kweneng Agriculture Chamber of Commerce website will serve agricultural businesses by providing access to resources, membership directories, and networking opportunities within Botswana.

Site Purpose

The purpose of the Kweneng Agriculture Chamber of Commerce website is to:

  • Provide a directory of agricultural businesses in the Kweneng region
  • Share current market trends, events, and workshops relevant to the agriculture industry
  • Promote membership benefits and facilitate applications to the Chamber
  • Provide up-to-date weather forecasts relevant to local agriculture

Scenarios

  1. A business owner wanting to join the Chamber: They can navigate to the "Join" page to review membership levels, apply, and learn about the benefits.
  2. A resident looking for agricultural events: They can access the "Discover" section to check for upcoming workshops, market days, and community agriculture events.

Typography

The site uses the Montserrat font family from Google Fonts. It will be applied as follows:

  • Headings: Montserrat Bold (700)
  • Body Text: Montserrat Regular (400)

This font was chosen for its modern and clean appearance, suitable for a professional and easy-to-navigate site.
