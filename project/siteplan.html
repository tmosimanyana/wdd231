Kweneng Agriculture Chamber of Commerce - Website Plan

Site Name

Site Name: Kweneng Agriculture Chamber of Commerce

Reason for Name: This site is for an agriculture chamber of commerce that promotes agricultural development and business growth in Kweneng, Botswana. The name reflects the geographical area and purpose of the chamber.

Domain Suggestion: kwenengagriculture.org

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to act as an information hub for the Kweneng Agriculture Chamber of Commerce. The website will offer resources for farmers, businesses, and the general public. It will include a business directory, chamber membership options, and educational materials on agricultural development.

Scenarios

  • Scenario 1: What are the benefits of becoming a member of the Kweneng Agriculture Chamber of Commerce?
  • Scenario 2: How can I find local businesses and agricultural services in Kweneng?

Color Schema

The following colors have been selected to align with the agricultural theme and nature:

Dark Green (#00582D): This color will be used for headings and buttons to symbolize growth and nature.
Light Gray (#E5E4E2): This color will be used for background and body text to ensure readability and a clean look.
